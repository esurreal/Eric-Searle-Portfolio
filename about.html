A little about me.

My name is Eric and I live in Atlanta Georgia. I've been making web creations since the early 2000's. I started with Adobe Flash and Dreamweaver making fun little websites for friends. In 2004 I was hired as a contractor for a startup called Eleven. The company was making language learning programs for kids using Flash. The project eventually got bought out and I moved into a different field. In 2020 the pandemic hit and I began looking for ways to use my time while the world was shut down. I decided to get back into web design/development and started learning Webflow. I immediately fell in love with the program. It feels right to me and I enjoy the workflow and layout. I've been designing and developing websites for clients using Webflow, Github, and VS Code ever since.

Other than web stuff, I enjoy playing and creating music, epic hiking, swimming, travel, good food (doesn't have to be expensive), cycling, and my cats.
